Lt.General Anil Chauhan

Former Eastern Army Commander Lt. General Anil
Chauhan , 61, is appointed as the next Chief of Defense
Staff (CDS) by the Government of India.

He was respected with many medals.
They are:

  • Param Vishisht Seva Medal
  • Uttam Yudh Seva Medal
  • Ati Vishisht Seva Medal
  • Sena Medal
  • Seva Medal
